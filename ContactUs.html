Our Vision

Butterflies are distinctive and popular insects with their slim bodies and large colorful wings. There are thousands of different species of butterfly and they can be found all over the world. Many adult butterflies play an important role in pollinating plants. However, there has been a decrease in the number of butterflies in the UK. Furthermore, there is growing evidence that this is a global problem, with a decline in the number of butterflies in many countries across the world.
